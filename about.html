About Us

All dreams can be possibilites once you put your mind to it. And in 2019, that very thought inspired Purrfect Match creator Grant Mak to launch this service.

Why do humans have to be the only ones with dating services? If we can be paired with someone based on personality, then why late fate leave it up to our precious cats? They deserve fullfilling and wholesome relationships just as much as their owners.

With our unique algorithm, we will pair your cat with its soul mate - guranteed. No one, even a cat, deserves to live a life alone. This is one step closer to achieving that dream.

Durham based Purrfect Match launched in 2019. It instantly became a hit, amassing over a million users in just a few months. With its unique pairing algorithm, members of Purrfect Match are matched to create long-lasting relationships.

Purrfect Match is available everywhere. Even if thousands of miles separate two cats, we promise that nothing will come in the way of your cat's Purrfect Match. If it's meant to be, it's meant to be: we are dedicated to making that happen.
